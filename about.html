About

This website was made to serve the purpose of sharing some of the best photographs taken within the Wellington region. The photographs include a range of different subjects, such as: landscapes, animals, sunsets and housing. The photographs aim to add an element of awe and to provide a brief insight into what it's like, in terms of scenery, to live in Wellington.
