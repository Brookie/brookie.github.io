The Rescues

Here is a small collection of some Wellington-based animal rescues. They all work tirelessly and effortlessly to help improve the lives of animals that they come across; they care for, love unconditionally and eventually help them meet their future family. Relying on donations and the adoptions to see them and their animals through the days, it's fair to say that the rescues and rescue-workers are a blessing for the animals.

SPCA Wellington

The Wellington SPCA

Wellington SPCA was founded in 1884, and to date their chain of rescues is the only approved non-government organisation that is able to enforce the Animal Welfare Act 1999- so it's fair to say they are angels to the animals they rescues. They have a variety of animals available to view and adopt, from cuddly kittens to adorable puppies and, of course, the fluffy rabbits and guinea pigs! Along with their animal rescues, they also have veterinary clinics and OP-shops.

They're located at 140 Alexandra Road in Newtown and are open Monday to Sunday from 10am to 4pm.

HUHANZ

Helping You Help Animals (HUHA)

As New Zealand's leading 'no kill' shelter that opened in 2001, HUHA strives to provide shelter for animals in need. They hope to teach communities empathy while nourishing animals in need and providing an environment where they can thrivea and grow. Their sancutaries have interactive and fun ways aimed at getting communities more involved in what's going on around them and to help out animals in need.

You can visit them at 61 Te Roto Rd in Otaki between the hours of 12pm to 3pm any day of the week.
