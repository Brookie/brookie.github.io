Our Aim

Link

This website is dedicated to helping prospective owners connect with rescues to find their future animal baby. We aim to help connect humans looking for one (or more!!) animal buddies with appropriate rescues to promote you to adopt rather than shop, or buy from a breeder. Adopting an animal not only brings joy into your life, but adds purpose and meaning to theirs!

Wellington has a large range of local rescues, all which have many animals in need! So why fall in love with a friend who happens to be on the other side of the country when the 'one' you're looking for could be right under your nose? Visit our Rescues page to see some of them, and learn some information about what they do!

Like any good animal-related website, we also boast an image gallery full of pictures of cute cats and delightful dogs that come from all over New Zealand! If you're more interested in meeting the Wellington local animals, we can point you in the right direction to that too!
